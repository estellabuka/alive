About Julius

My journey is deeply connected with the idea of becoming fully alive. As a leadership coach, I help individuals develop profound self-awareness, build authentic connections, and make a lasting impact in their personal and professional lives. My approach to embodied leadership is rooted in modern Gestalt therapy, bodywork and neuroscience, ensuring that leaders integrate their body intelligence in their day-to-day leadership.

After working in management consulting and leadership roles within the e-commerce and tech industries, I realized the importance of merging leadership consulting with humanistic psychology, neuroscience, and somatic coaching. I co-authored *Self-Empowered Leadership*, a book designed to help leaders enhance their satisfaction and influence within organizations. However, I felt that something was missing—we needed a dedicated space to bring our vision to life. And so, Alive School was born.

Alive School is more than a training center—it’s a movement toward holistic, embodied leadership. By integrating mind and body, we help professionals unlock their potential and create meaningful change in their organizations and beyond.
