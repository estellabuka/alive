The Alive School Community Space

Connect, Recreate, Lead

3-day leadership offsite in a wonderful atmosphere at the countryside near Berlin; time to connect with yourself and to recharge your batteries; exchange with other leaders; explore new solutions for your leadership challenges; the latest in leadership, neuropsychology and bodywork

Goals and benefits of the leadership offsite

The Alive School Community Space takes place three times a year and helps leaders to connect with their aliveness and to encounter their leadership challenges in a generative and energizing way:

  • Connect with yourself on a deeper level: Accept your inner devils and commit to your purpose in leadership and life.
  • Work holistically on your mental and physical health to ensure long-term perforance, wellbeing and satisfaction.
  • Increase your impact in your personal and professional life by living up to your your values through powerful embodiment practices and routines.
  • Build a network of likeminded people that meet as both – professionals and human beings.
  • Get access to exclusive events for community members and receive the latest news and updates on emobided leadership & transformation.

The Alive School Community Space takes place near Berlin over three days, starting on the first day at 3 pm and ending on the third day at 1 pm. The facilitator for both days is Julius Lassalle. On the second day one session is offered by an additional trainer:

  • October 5-7, 2025 : Wim Hoff breathing session by Peter Panda
  • January 29-31, 2026: Body combat exercise session by Eda Example

The offsite takes place in Neupitz, a beautiful location in the middle of nature and by the lake with the motto "Work-Lake-Balance". Neupitz can be reached quickly from Berlin and is also perfectly connected to BER airport, which is only 20 minutes away.

Key content of the leadership offsite

Leadership is always embedded - in the situation of the company, but also in the respective life situation of the individual leader:

  • The retreat provides a space to connect with the very personal situation of every leader: Where do I stand in my leadership and in my life? What issues do I want to work on for myself to feel more alive?
  • The Alive School Community Space consists of five different types of sessions:
    • Mindfulness and other bodywork sessions in which you can get in touch with yourself and your questions.
    • Guide group coaching sessions with the facilitator in which you can further deepen your topic and integrate mind and body in the sense of the embodiment approach – including deep dives in embodiment research and methodology.
    • Mini workshops on special leadership and embodiment topics provided by Julius Lassalle or an additional trainer.
    • Peer coaching sessions in which you can discuss relevant leadership topics of your interest with other participants.
    • Individual space that you can use as you need it, e.g. for walks, relaxation phases or personal dialogue with other p..

The following aspects are part of our Alive School:

  1. All participants are sent a questionnaire in advance to help them identify their personal concerns for the leadership retreat. Answering the questions is completely voluntary and sometimes the really important questions and concerns only come to light during the retreat.
  2. All (bodywork) methods are always explained against the background of a humanistic understanding of leadership and coaching and linked to models from the fields of neuroscience, philosophy and psychology. Depending on the interests of the participants, we go into varying depths here.
  3. All participants are actively encouraged to take responsibility for their own wishes and needs during the retreat, i.e. to pursue their own concerns or to incorporate sufficient recovery phases and to skip sessions for this purpose.
  4. The leadership offsite is an experimental space in which participants can test their limits. The motto is "everyone goes as far as they want and as far as they can"!
  5. Just as central as the individual and joint experimentation is the encounter between the participants - professionally in exchange as managers and personally as people.

Who is the leadership offsite near Berlin for?

The training is aimed at leaders in organisations, consultants and professionals that are interested in a new, powerful type of leadership:

  • who want to bring more mindfulness into their everyday management work and achieve more inner balance;
  • who believe in holistic leadership, i.e. leadership that is not only controlled by the head, but also by our intuition, in which rational solutions must always be a human fit as well
  • who are keen to experiment and explore thei aliveness in a vibrant, trusting atmosphere

Please note: The Leadership Retreat requires a certain level of physical and mental health. If you currently have restrictions on either of these levels, please contact us. We will be happy to clarify in a preliminary discussion whether and when participation makes sense.

Methods

Our starting point for all exercises and sessions is always the personal experience, namely "from the body to the head". We therefore start with the personal emotional and physical experience, reflect on it and apply it to the respective leadership and life worlds of the participants. In contrast to most conventional training programmes, learning at the Alive School is always holistic, i.e:

  • with the head/mind: models and theoretical input, self-reflection
  • with the body/heart: individual and group exercises, body hacks
  • at the behavioural level: simulations, routines, action plans
